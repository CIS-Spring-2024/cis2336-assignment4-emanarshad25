Owner's Biography

Welcome to our cafe! I am Eman Arshad, the proud owner of the UH Sugar Cafe. With over 10 years of experience in the culinary world, my team and I are dedicated to bringing you the best dining experience.

Sugar Cafe is located in the UH Sugar Land cafeteria. Our main purpose to establish this cafe was to provide students a place they can hang out with friends and enjoy good food.

As being a student myself back in the day, having a place where one student can study, hang out, or just eat is essential. Here at Sugar Cafe we not only serve food but help make our students' time here memorable!
